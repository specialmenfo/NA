隱私政策 - Privacy Policy：

我們十分重視您的隱私，並致力於保護您的個人資訊。請仔細閱讀以下政策，了解我們如何收集、使用、披露和保護您的個人資訊。

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. Please read the following policy carefully to understand how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information.

收集的信息 - Information Collected：

我們可能收集的信息包括但不限於：姓名、電子郵件地址、郵寄地址、電話號碼、付款信息等。我們收集這些信息是為了確保訂單的完成和溝通的順利進行。

The information we may collect includes, but is not limited to, names, email addresses, mailing addresses, phone numbers, payment information, etc. We collect this information to ensure the completion of orders and smooth communication.

使用目的 - Purpose of Use：

收集的信息將用於處理訂單、提供客戶服務、改善我們的產品和服務、以及向您提供有關特定產品或服務的信息。

The collected information will be used for order processing, providing customer service, improving our products and services, and providing you with information about specific products or services.

披露對象及披露方式 - Disclosure Recipients and Methods：

我們不會將您的個人資訊出售、交易或出租給第三方。我們僅在必要時與第三方服務提供商分享信息，以協助我們完成上述目的。

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share information with third-party service providers when necessary to assist us in achieving the aforementioned purposes.

取消政策 - Cancellation Policy：

如果您希望取消訂單或刪除您的個人資訊，請在收到訂單確認後的最短時間內與我們聯繫。我們將盡快處理您的請求，但請注意，某些信息可能會保留用於記錄或法律要求。

If you wish to cancel an order or delete your personal information, please contact us as soon as possible after receiving the order confirmation. We will process your request promptly, but please note that certain information may be retained for recordkeeping or legal requirements.

保護措施 - Protection Measures：

我們採取合理的技術和組織措施來保護您的個人資訊免受未經授權的訪問、使用、披露、更改或破壞。

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

如果您對我們的隱私政策有任何疑問或疑慮，請隨時與我們聯繫。

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please feel free to contact us.
